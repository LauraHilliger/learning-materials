Story in a Tweet

Writing a long letter is much easier than writing a short letter. This activity gets to the heart of what the Greenpeace story is about. Once people have had the chance to play with and take the story for a test drive then this is a great way of crystallizing what itâ€™s really about.

Made by Lucy and Laura

Steps for the Activity

  1. Present the Greenpeace Story arc to the group or give them the chance to look at it at their leisure.
  2. Having heard the story and understood its parts, how would you summarise it if you had to write it in a tweet?
  3. Have people read their tweets, or better still get them to post them and watch on screen as they come through. Use the hashtag #courageis

Materials

  • Post its
  • Phones for tweeting
  • Markers and pens
  • Laptop
  • Projector

Discussion

How does it feel to be so constrained? Do these tweets feel inspiring to you? Do they ignite courage in others?
